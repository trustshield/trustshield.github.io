Identify Risks Early

TrustShield is designed to identify potential risks early in the development process, allowing your team to take a structured and disciplined approach to mitigating these risks before final product release.

Mitigation Strategies

TrustShield provides a range of mitigation strategies to address each identified risk. We offer solutions for data encryption, data leakage prevention, data access controls, and compliance regulations, making sure you're on the right track for developing a compliant and secure product.

Seamless Integration

TrustShield seamlessly integrates with project management tools and your development stack and code repositories. We also offer a plugin for Jira and Linear, so developers and project managers can quickly get feedback on potential risks without ever leaving their established workflow.
